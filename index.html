Cast Iron Manufacturer in Rajkot, Gujarat

Asian Technocast is a trusted cast iron manufacturer and CI casting supplier in Rajkot, Gujarat, established in 2006. Located in the heart of Aji GIDC, Rajkot — one of India's most productive engineering industrial estates — we have spent nearly two decades perfecting the art of precision cast iron manufacturing.

We are specialists in electric motor body castings, grey iron castings, ductile iron castings, and a full range of CI casting components for automotive, agricultural, and industrial sectors. Our castings conform to IS 210 (FG 200, FG 260, FG 300) and IS 1536 standards with multi-stage quality inspection.

As a leading cast iron foundry in Rajkot, we offer drawing-to-casting manufacturing — ideal for OEM suppliers, motor manufacturers, pump makers, and engineering companies seeking reliable, precision cast iron components with Pan-India supply capability.

CI Casting Products We Supply

  • ▸Motor Body Castings (Frame 56–355) – stator housing, motor frame, end bell for 3-phase & single-phase motors
  • ▸End Shield Castings – drive end (DE) and non-drive end (NDE) shields with precision bearing bore
  • ▸Gear Housing & Gearbox Castings – high-strength IS 210 FG 300 for industrial gearboxes
  • ▸Cylinder Head Castings – thermally stable, pressure-tested for diesel & petrol engines
  • ▸Water Pump & Volute Castings – corrosion-resistant pump bodies for agricultural & industrial pumps
  • ▸Automobile Castings – brake components, hubs, and precision OEM automotive parts
  • ▸Agriculture Castings – wear-resistant components for tractors, ploughs, and field machinery
  • ▸Custom CI Castings – manufactured to your drawings in grey iron, ductile iron, or alloy grades
